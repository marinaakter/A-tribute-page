About the Legend

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam (1931–2015), widely known as A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and went on to become a key figure in India's civilian space program and military missile development, earning him the nickname "Missile Man of India."
